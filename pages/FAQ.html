Orders & Payment

You can track your order in several ways:

  • Use the tracking number provided in your confirmation email
  • Log into your account and view order history
  • Visit our Order Tracking page
  • Contact our support team for assistance

Tracking information is usually available within 24-48 hours after your order ships.

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Debit Cards
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
  • Buy Now, Pay Later options (Klarna, Afterpay)

Order modifications depend on the processing status:

  • Before processing: Contact us immediately to cancel or modify
  • During processing: Changes may not be possible
  • After shipping: You can return items following our return policy

Contact our support team as soon as possible for the best chance of modification.

Shipping & Delivery

Shipping times vary by location and method:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days
  • Next-Day Delivery: Available in select areas
  • International: 7-14 business days

Processing time is typically 1-2 business days before shipping.

Our shipping rates are:

  • Free Standard Shipping: Orders over $75
  • Standard Shipping: $5.99
  • Express Shipping: $12.99
  • Next-Day Delivery: $19.99

International shipping costs vary by destination and are calculated at checkout.

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International customers should note:

  • Shipping times: 7-14 business days
  • Customs fees may apply (customer responsibility)
  • Some products may have shipping restrictions
  • Return shipping costs are customer responsibility

Returns & Exchanges

Our return policy includes:

  • Return Window: 30 days from delivery date
  • Condition: Items must be unworn with original tags
  • Refund Method: Original payment method
  • Processing Time: 3-5 business days after we receive your return

Some items like intimate apparel and sale items may have different return policies.

Yes! We offer exchanges within 30 days. You can:

  • Exchange for different size (subject to availability)
  • Exchange for different color (subject to availability)
  • Pay/receive refund for price differences

Start your exchange request through your account or contact our support team.

Return shipping costs depend on the reason:

  • Our error or defect: We provide free return shipping
  • Change of mind: Customer pays return shipping ($6.99)
  • Wrong size: Free return shipping for first exchange
  • International returns: Customer responsibility

Sizing & Fit

Finding your perfect size is easy:

  • Use our comprehensive Size Guide
  • Check the size chart on each product page
  • Read customer reviews for fit feedback
  • Contact us for personalized sizing advice

When in doubt, we recommend ordering your usual size and taking advantage of our free exchange policy.

Most of our items run true to size, but fit can vary by:

  • Brand: Some brands may run larger or smaller
  • Style: Oversized pieces are noted in descriptions
  • Fabric: Stretchy materials may fit differently

Check the "Fit & Sizing" section on each product page and read customer reviews for the most accurate fit information.

Account & Profile

Creating an account is quick and easy:

  • Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation
  • Enter your email address and create a password
  • Verify your email address
  • Complete your profile information

Account benefits include faster checkout, order tracking, exclusive offers, and wishlist functionality.

To reset your password:

  • Go to the login page
  • Click "Forgot Password?"
  • Enter your email address
  • Check your email for reset instructions
  • Follow the link to create a new password

If you don't receive the email within 10 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

You can manage your email preferences by:

  • Logging into your account and visiting "Email Preferences"
  • Clicking "Unsubscribe" at the bottom of any email
  • Contacting our support team

You can choose to receive promotional emails, order updates, or both.
